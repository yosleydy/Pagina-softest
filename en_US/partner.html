Grupo A&E Softest gives our clients and people the opportunity to take a new step in their professional training with our program of specialized training courses in Testing.

Our instructors have extensive experience and certificates in the area of ​​Software Quality.


ISTQB Certified Tester Foundation Level – Versión 2.018

Duration: 18 hours

Objectives:

    • Use a common language for efficient and effective communication with other testers.
    • Understand concepts such as: test process, test approaches, principles to support test objectives.
    • Design and prioritize tests by using techniques; analyze functional and non-functional specifications, at all levels of testing.
    • Execute tests according to test plans, and analyze and report results.
    • Write clear and understandable incident reports.
    • Know different types of testing tools and their uses; assist in the selection process.

Addressed to:

    • QA Consultants
    • QA Leaders
    • Developers
    • Professionals who wish to gain insight into the testing process

Contents:

The Course is oriented according to the ISTQB curriculum, addressing the following topics:

    • Chapter I. Fundamentals of the Testing Process
    • Chapter II. Testing Throughout the Software Development Lifecycle
    • Chapter III. Static Test
    • Chapter IV – Test Techniques
    • Chapter V – Test Management
    • Chapter VI. Tool Support for the Testing Process

             It includes:

    • Support Material (Manual, Exercises and Exams)
    • Mock exams at the end of each chapter.
    • Mock exam at the end of the course (40 questions in 60 minutes)
    • International Certification Exam ISTQB Certified Tester – Foundation Level (CTFL) (Optional)

Certification:

ISQI certifications create security for professionals and companies, because it is the leading Provider of certification exams worldwide.
ISQI certified professionals develop exceptional careers, provide a high quality structured service, show commitment to professional development and discipline.
